ERP system for SHIMMERS Company

Inventory Management System for Shimmers Company This project is a comprehensive inventory management solution developed for Shimmers Company to streamline and modernize their stock control operations. The system enables efficient order generation, maintains detailed stock records, and incorporates intelligent tracking of expiry dates to minimize losses and ensure product safety. A built-in notification alert mechanism proactively informs users of critical updates, such as low stock levels or upcoming expirations. Additionally, the system supports advanced reporting features and leverages Recharts to deliver dynamic, data-driven visualizations for improved decision-making.

Personal AI Chatbot – Intelligent Conversational Assistant

This project introduces a next-generation personal AI assistant designed to deliver natural, context-aware conversations with a sleek, modern user interface.
